Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Flyvevejr respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

Flyvevejr may request access to your device location in order to retrieve local weather forecasts.

The app does not create user accounts, store your location, sell personal information, use advertising tracking, or share personal information with third parties.

Location Data

Location access is used only to fetch weather data for your current area. Your location is not stored by Flyvevejr.
